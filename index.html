Privacy Policy for HaircutLog

Last updated: July 11, 2026

HaircutLog is a simple personal app for logging haircut dates and photos.

What data the app collects

  • Photos you choose to add (from your camera or photo library) when logging a haircut.
  • Dates you assign to each haircut entry.

How your data is stored

All data you enter is stored using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) private database, tied to your own Apple ID. This means:

  • Your data is stored in your own iCloud account, not on any server operated by the developer.
  • The developer of HaircutLog cannot see, access, or collect your photos or data.
  • Data automatically syncs across your own devices that are signed into the same Apple ID via Apple's iCloud service.

Data sharing

HaircutLog does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Data deletion

You can delete any entry at any time within the app. Deleting the app and disabling iCloud sync for it (in iOS Settings) removes the data from your device and stops future syncing.
